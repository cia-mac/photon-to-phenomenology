the rows look wedged
what this is
Offset rows of light and dark tiles with thin grey mortar make the perfectly level lines look sloped and wedged. The contrast at each tile edge tugs the perceived line.
what to do
Drag sideways to change the row offset. Slide it to zero and every row snaps level. Or ▶ walk me through it.
ⓘ about
Every horizontal line is exactly level and parallel. Slide the shift to zero and you can see it. Offset tiles and thin grey mortar make your visual system tilt the edges. The slope is added by you. Vision is not recording, it is construction.
