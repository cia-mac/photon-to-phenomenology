what this is
Adapt to steady motion and the detectors tuned to it tire out. When it stops, their silence reads as motion the other way. This is the motion aftereffect, or waterfall illusion.
what to do
Drag sideways to spin the spiral for ~20s while you fixate the centre, then drag it back to a stop and keep looking.
ⓘ about
Held still, the spiral keeps breathing, outward or inward, against the way you spun it. Your motion detectors adapted to the steady flow, and their silence now reads as motion the other way. Vision is not recording, it is construction.
