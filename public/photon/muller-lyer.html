the top shaft looks longer
what this is
The two shafts are the same length. Fins splayed outward make one look longer; fins folded inward make the other look shorter, read as near and far corners.
what to do
Drag to fold the fins in and out. Flatten them and the shafts line up.
ⓘ about
The two horizontal shafts are identical in length. Fold the fins flat and they line up exactly. Fins splaying out say "near corner," fins folding in say "far corner," and your visual system scales the line to fit. Vision is not recording, it is construction.
