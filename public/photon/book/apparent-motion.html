what this is
Two lamps flashing in turn, with nothing between them, are seen as one object crossing the gap. At the right rhythm your visual system builds motion no object ever made (phi).

ⓘ about
Two lamps blinking in turn. At the right rhythm you saw one thing cross the gap, but nothing ever did. The motion was built by you, from two still flashes. Vision is not recording, it is construction.
