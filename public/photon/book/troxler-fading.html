Hold still and the ring leaves

what this is
Hold your gaze still and unchanging things in the periphery fade from awareness. Steady, unvarying input stops being reported (Troxler fading).
what to do
Stare at the centre cross without moving your eyes. Drag to widen the ring; flick your eyes to bring it back.
ⓘ about
The ring is still on the screen. Flick your eyes and it returns at full strength. Steady, unchanging input stops being reported. Your visual system spends its bandwidth on change, and erases the rest. Vision is not recording, it is construction.
