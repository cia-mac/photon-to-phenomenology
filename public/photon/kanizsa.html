A triangle that isn't there
inducersaligned
a bright triangle, floating
what this is
Three discs, each with a wedge cut out. When the wedges point inward you see a solid triangle with sharp, bright edges lying on top. Yet no triangle, no edge and no brighter surface is ever drawn.
what to do
Drag to rotate the wedges. Watch the triangle sharpen into being, then dissolve. Or ▶ walk me through it.
ⓘ about
There are three notched discs and nothing else. No triangle is drawn, no edges, no brighter surface. When the notches line up your visual system builds the simplest cause: an opaque triangle lying on top. The edges are yours. Vision is not recording, it is construction.
