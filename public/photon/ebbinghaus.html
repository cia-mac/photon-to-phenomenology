what this is
The two centre discs are exactly the same size, but the one ringed by giants looks smaller than the one ringed by dwarves. Size is judged against the neighbours, not measured.
what to do
Drag to grow and shrink the surrounding rings. The centre discs never change.
ⓘ about
The two centre discs are exactly the same size. Drag the rings together and you can finally see it. Size is judged against the neighbours, not measured. The same disc shrinks beside giants and swells beside dwarves. Vision is not recording, it is construction.
